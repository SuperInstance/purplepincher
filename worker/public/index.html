How It Works

1. Pick a prompt (or write your own) → 2. Choose a strategy → 3. Watch the rounds → 4. Get a refined answer

Instead of one-shot, the AI goes through multiple rounds of structured thinking. Each round challenges the answer from a different angle. The final result is always better than the first draft.

The trick: Copy the prompts from each round into ANY AI chatbot (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Grok, etc.) and paste the responses back here. You're literally watching the machine learn in real-time.
